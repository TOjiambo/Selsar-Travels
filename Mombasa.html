Mombasa Raha

Day 1:

Upon your arrival at Moi International Airport, our friendly team will greet you and transfer you to your luxurious beachfront resort. Settle into your accommodation and spend the day at leisure, unwinding by the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean or exploring the resort's amenities.

Day 2:


Embark on a thrilling marine safari to the nearby marine park, where you'll discover the underwater wonders of the Indian Ocean. Dive into the crystal-clear waters for a snorkeling adventure, encountering colorful coral reefs, tropical fish, and marine turtles. Afterward, enjoy a beachside barbecue lunch before returning to your resort.

Day 3:


Embark on a guided tour of Mombasa's highlights, starting with a visit to Fort Jesus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site steeped in history. Explore the fort's architecture, museums, and panoramic views of the coastline. Continue to the Old Town, known for its Swahili architecture, bustling markets, and vibrant atmosphere.


Day 4:


Today is yours to tailor as you wish. Relax at the resort, soaking in the sun and sea breeze, or choose from a range of optional excursions:

Dolphin watching tour

Visit to Haller Park for wildlife encounters

Day 5:


On your final day, savor a leisurely breakfast and perhaps take a final stroll along the pristine beaches of Mombasa.
