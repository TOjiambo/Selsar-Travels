3-Day Snorkeling

Day 1:

Arrive at the coastal paradise of Watamu and be greeted by our friendly team. Transfer to your beachfront resort, where the azure waters beckon you. After settling in, spend the afternoon at leisure, soaking up the sun-kissed shores and savoring fresh seafood delights.

Day 2:


Gear up for an unforgettable snorkeling expedition to Watamu National Park. Board a boat with experienced guides who will navigate to the most pristine coral reefs teeming with colorful fish, sea turtles, and mesmerizing underwater landscapes. Dive into the crystal-clear waters and marvel at the biodiversity that surrounds you.

Day 3:


On your final day, seize the opportunity for more snorkeling escapades or choose to explore the Malindi coast, adding cultural richness to your coastal retreat. After a delectable farewell lunch, we bid you adieu with cherished memories of the ocean's wonders.


Package Inclusions:


Accommodation at a beachfront resort

Daily breakfast and beachside meals

Guided snorkeling excursions

Snorkeling gear and safety equipment
